    ![[<span data-ttu-id="3bc70-108">Sisseelamisjuhendi jagamine teiste kaasautoritega</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3bc70-108">Sharing an onboarding guide with other contributors</span></span>](./media/onboard-share-guide.png)](./media/onboard-share-guide.png)

4. <span data-ttu-id="3bc70-112">Sisestage nimi või meiliaadress ja valige ripploendist soovitud adressaat.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3bc70-112">Enter a name or email address, and select the desired recipient in the drop-down list.</span></span>
