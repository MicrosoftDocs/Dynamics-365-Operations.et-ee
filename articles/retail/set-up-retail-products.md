# <a name="set-up-retail-products"></a><span data-ttu-id="2cd07-103">Jaetoodete häälestamine</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-103">Set up retail products</span></span>

[!include[banner](includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="2cd07-104">Selles artiklis kirjeldatakse, kuidas seadistada jaetooteid rakenduses Microsoft Dynamics 365 for Retail.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-104">This article describes how to set up retail products in Microsoft Dynamics 365 for Retail.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2cd07-105">Enne kui saate oma jaemüügikanalitel hakata tooteid edasimüügiks pakkuma, peate tooted looma ja konfigureerima rakenduses Dynamics 365 for Retail.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-105">Before you can offer products for resale in your retail channels, you must create and configure the products in Dynamics 365 for Retail.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-106">Jaemüük kasutab rakenduses Dynamics 365 for Retail olevaid tootefunktsioone, et luua tooteetalonis kogu organisatsiooni hõlmavaid tooteid.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-106">Retail uses the product features in Dynamics 365 for Retail to create organization-wide products in the product master.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-107">Saate luua tooted, määratleda toote omadused ja atribuudid ning määrata tooted jaemüügikategooria hierarhiatesse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-107">You can create the products, define the product properties and attributes, and assign the products to retail category hierarchies.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-108">Selleks et teha tooted jaemüügikanalites kättesaadavaks ja lisada need aktiivsele sortimendile, peate tooted väljastama juriidilistele isikutele, kus need on saadaval.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-108">To make the products available to your retail channels and add them to an active assortment, you must release the products to the legal entities where they are available.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-109">Jaemüügikanalite kaudu müüdavate toodete seadistamiseks täitke järgmised ülesanded.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-109">To set up the products that you sell by using retail channels, complete the following tasks.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-110">Määratlege jaemüügi tootehierarhia.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-110">Define a retail product hierarchy.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-111">Rakenduses Dynamics 365 for Retail olevate kategooriahierarhiate funktsioonide kasutamisel saate määratleda jaemüügikategooria hierarhiad, et grupeerida ja kategoriseerida jaemüügikanalitesse levitatavaid tooteid.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-111">By using the category hierarchy features in Dynamics 365 for Retail, you can define retail category hierarchies to group and categorize the products that you distribute to your retail channels.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-112">Kasutaja määratletavaid ja süsteemi atribuute saab määratleda kategooria tasemel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-112">User-defined and system attributes can be defined at the category level.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-113">Seejärel, pärivad kõik kategooriale määratud tooted need atribuudid.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-113">Then, all products that are assigned to the category inherit those attributes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-114">Määratleda saab mitu kategooriahierarhiat ja iga toote saab määrata mitmele hierarhiale.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-114">Multiple category hierarchies can be defined, and each product can be assigned to multiple hierarchies.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-115">Ühes hierarhias saab siiski iga toote määrata ainult ühte kategooriasse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-115">However, in a single hierarchy, each product can be assigned to only one category.</span></span>
2.  <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-116">Lisage tooted ja tootevariandid tooteetalonile.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-116">Add products and product variants to the product master.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-117">Tooteetalonile lisatud tooted esindavad toodete globaalset loendit.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-117">Products that are added to the product master represent a global list of products.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-118">Saate lisada tooteid käsitsi, ükshaaval või importida toote andmed hankijatelt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-118">You can add products manually, one at a time, or you can import product data from your vendors.</span></span>
3.  <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-119">Väljastage tooted juriidilistele isikutele.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-119">Release the products to legal entities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-120">Jaemüügikanalitele saab kättesaadavaks teha ainult juriidilistele isikutele väljastatud tooteid.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-120">Only products that have been released to legal entities can be made available to your retail channels.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-121">Toote algsel määratlemisel määratlege see organisatsiooniülesel tasemel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-121">When you first define a product, you define it on an organization-wide level.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-122">Seejärel saate valida ühe või mitu juriidilist isikut, kellele toode väljastada.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-122">You can then select one or more legal entities to release the product to.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-123">Siis muutub toode kättesaadavaks teie organisatsiooni mitmele jaemüügikanalile.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-123">The product then becomes available to multiple retail channels across your organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-124">Selle funktsiooni abil saate luua toote ühekorraga, lisada ning värskendada toote atribuute ja omadusi ühes kohas ning seejärel levitada toodet kogu organisatsioonis jaemüügikanalitesse, kus see on saadaval.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-124">You can use this functionality to create a product one time, add and update product attributes and properties in one place, and then distribute the product across your organization, to the retail channels where it's available.</span></span>
4.  <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-125">Lisage tooted sortimentidesse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-125">Add products to assortments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-126">Sortiment kujutab endast teie jaemüügikanalites pakutavate toodete kogumit.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-126">An assortment represents a collection of products that you offer in your retail channels.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-127">Saate määratleda ühe või mitu sortimenti ja iga toote saab määrata ühte või mitmesse sortimenti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-127">You can define one or more assortments, and each product can be assigned to one or more assortments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-128">Toodete määramiseks jaemüügikanalitele määrake sortimentid nende jaemüügikanalitele.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-128">To assign products to retail channels, you assign the assortments to those retail channels.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-129">Sortimendi loomisel saate lisada tooteid, mida pole veel juriidilisele isikule väljastatud.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-129">When you create an assortment, you can add products that haven't yet been released to a legal entity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-130">Siiski peate tooted väljastama juriidilisele isikule enne, kui need saab jaemüügikanalitele kättesaadavaks teha.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-130">However, you must release the products to a legal entity before those products can be made available to the retail channels.</span></span>
5.  <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-131">Lisage tooted navigeerimishierarhiatele.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-131">Add products to navigation hierarchies.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-132">Enne kui tooteid saab võrgus või kassas sirvida, tuleb need kategoriseerida jaemüügi navigeerimishierarhias.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-132">Before products can be browsed online or in point of sale (POS), they must be categorized in a Retail navigation hierarchy.</span></span>
6.  <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-133">Lisage kataloogidesse tooted.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-133">Add products to catalogs.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2cd07-134">Kuigi see juhis on kassa jaoks valikuline, nõuavad võrgupoed, et tooteid kaasataks vähemalt ühte kataloogi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2cd07-134">Although this step is optional for POS, online stores require that products be included in at least one catalog.</span></span>
