# <a name="set-up-rfm-analysis"></a><span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-103">RFM-analüüsi seadistamine</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-103">Set up RFM analysis</span></span>

[!include[banner](includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-104">Selles teemas selgitatakse, kuidas seadistada klientidele hiljutisuse, sageduse ja valuuta (RFM-i) analüüsi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-104">This topic explains how to set up a Recency, Frequency, and Monetary (RFM) analysis of your customers.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-105">Hiljutisuse, sageduse ja rahasumma (RFM) analüüs on turunduse tööriist, mida teie organisatsioon saab kasutada kliendi ostudega loodud andmete hindamiseks.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-105">Recency, frequency, and monetary (RFM) analysis is a marketing tool that your organization can use to evaluate the data that is generated by customer purchases.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-106">Pärast RFM-analüüsi seadistamist määratakse klientidele arvutatud RFM-i skoori, kui nad oste sooritavad.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-106">After you set up RFM analysis, customers are assigned a calculated RFM score as they make purchases.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-107">RFM-i skoor võib olla kolmekohaline hinne või koondnumber, sõltuvalt sellest, kuidas teie organisatsioon on RFM-analüüsi seadistanud.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-107">The RFM score can be a three-digit rating or an aggregate number, depending on how your organization has configured RFM analysis.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-108">Kui teie organisatsioon kasutab skoori puhul kolmekohalist hinnet, siis selle põhimõte on järgmine.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-108">Here’s how the rating works if your organization uses a three-digit rating for the score:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-110">Teine number on kliendi sageduse hinne, mis näitab, kui tihti klient teie organisatsioonist ostab.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-110">The second digit is the customer's frequency rating, which is how often the customer makes purchases from your organization.</span></span> 
- <span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-111">Kolmas number on kliendi rahasumma hinne, mis näitab, kui palju klient teie organisatsioonist ostude tegemisel kulutab.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-111">The third digit is the customer's monetary rating, which is how much the customer spends when he makes purchases from your organization.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-112">Näiteks on teie organisatsioon määranud hinded skaalal 1 kuni 5, kus 5 on kõrgeim hinne.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-112">For example, your organization has set the ratings on a scale of 1 through 5, where 5 is the highest rating.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-113">Sel juhul ütleb kliendi hinne 535 teile kliendi kohta järgmist.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-113">In this case, a customer rating of 535 tells you the following information about the customer:</span></span>

-   <span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-114">**Hiljutisuse hinne 5** – klient ostis hiljuti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-114">**Recency rating of 5** – The customer recently made a purchase.</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-115">**Sageduse hinne 3** – klient ostab teie organisatsioonist tooteid keskmise sagedusega.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-115">**Frequency rating of 3** – The customer purchases products from your organization moderately often.</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-116">**Rahasumma hinne 5** – klient kulutab ostes märkimisväärse rahasumma.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-116">**Monetary rating of 5** – When the customer makes a purchase, he spends a significant amount of money.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-117">Kui teie organisatsioon kasutab skoori puhul koondnumbrit, pannake üksikud hinded kokku.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-117">If your organization uses an aggregate number for the score, the individual ratings are added together.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-118">Sama näite puhul on kliendi hinne 13 (5 + 3 + 5).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-118">For the same example, the customer has a rating of 13 (5 + 3 + 5).</span></span>

    - <span data-ttu-id="7e7ff-126">Kui RFM-i skoori iga osa peab sisaldama võrdset arvu kliente, märkige ruut **Ühtlane jaotus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7e7ff-126">If each section of the RFM score must contain an equal count of customers, select the **Even distribution** check box.</span></span>
