## <a name="create-a-new-list"></a><span data-ttu-id="074af-106">Uue loendi loomine</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="074af-106">Create a new list</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="074af-107">Kureeritud toote soovituste loendi loomiseks tehke järgmist.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="074af-107">To create a curated product recommendation list, follow these steps.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="074af-108">Avage **Jaemüük ja kaubandus &gt; Tootesoovitused &gt; Soovituste loendid**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="074af-108">Go to **Retail and Commerce &gt; Product recommendations &gt; Recommendation lists**.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="074af-109">Valige suvand **Uus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="074af-109">Select **New**.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="074af-110">Sisestage väljal **Loendi ID** väärtus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="074af-110">In the **List Id** field, enter a value.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="074af-111">Sisestage väljal **Loendi nimi** väärtus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="074af-111">In the **List name** field, enter a value.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="074af-112">Suvand **Loendi nimi** on loendi pealkiri, mis ilmub mooduli **Toote kollektsioon** kureeritud loendite jaotises.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="074af-112">The **List name** is the title of the list that will appear in the curated lists section of the **Product collection** module.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="074af-113">Toodete loendisse lisamiseks valige suvand **Lisa tooted**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="074af-113">To add products to the list, select **Add products**.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="074af-114">Loendis toodete järjekorra muutmiseks sisestage väärtus veerus **Kuvamise järjestus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="074af-114">To change the order of the products in the list, enter a value in the **Display order** column.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="074af-115">Kui kahel tootel on sama kuvamise järjestuse väärtus, siis võib nende kahe tulemuse lõplik järjestus kontorist erineda.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="074af-115">If two products have the same display order value, then the final order of those two results may differ from the back office.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="074af-116">Loendi salvestamiseks valige **Salvesta**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="074af-116">Select **Save** to save the list.</span></span>
