[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

1. <span data-ttu-id="09e64-106">Minge jaotisse Pearaamat > Kontoplaan > Kontod > Pearaamatukonto pseudonüüm.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="09e64-106">Go to General ledger > Chart of accounts > Accounts > Ledger account alias.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="09e64-107">Klõpsake valikut Uus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="09e64-107">Click New.</span></span>
