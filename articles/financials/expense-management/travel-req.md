# <a name="travel-requisitions"></a><span data-ttu-id="eb564-104">Reisiplaanid</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb564-104">Travel requisitions</span></span>

[!INCLUDE [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="eb564-105">*Reisiplaan* on lähtedokument, mis loetleb reisimise eesmärgil tekkinud kulud.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb564-105">A *travel requisition* is a source document that lists the expenses that will be incurred for the purpose of travel.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eb564-106">Reisiplaan esitatakse ülevaatamiseks ja seejärel saab seda kasutada kulude kinnitamiseks.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb564-106">A travel requisition is submitted for review and can then be used to authorize expenses.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eb564-107">Teie organisatsioon võib nõuda reisiplaani esitamist enne kui töötajad teevad kulutusi, mille eest tasub organisatsioon.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb564-107">Your organization might require that a travel requisition be submitted before employees incur any expense that is charged to the organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eb564-108">See nõue kehtib olenemata sellest, kas töötaja maksab ettevõtte krediitkaardiga, kasutab sularaha ettemakse kaudu saadud sularaha või tasub tegelike kulude eest, mille hüvitab organisatsioon.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb564-108">This requirement applies, regardless of whether employees charge expenses to a corporate credit card, spend cash that they received from a cash advance, or incur out-of-pocket expenses that will be reimbursed by the organization.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eb564-109">Reisiplaane ja poliitikaid saab kasutada eelarve juhtimise juures.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb564-109">Travel requisitions and policies can be used to help with budget control.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eb564-110">Näiteks, kui teie organisatsioon töötab fikseeritud hinnaga projekti kallal, mille jaoks on vajalik reisimine, peavad projekti töörühma liikmete reisikulud mahtuma projekti eelarve piiridesse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb564-110">For example, if your organization is working on a fixed-price project that requires travel, the travel expenses of the project's team members must fit within the budget for the project.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eb564-111">Kui organisatsioon nõuab, et reisikulud tuleb kinnitada enne nende tekkimist, aitab see tagada, et projekt jääb eelarve piiridesse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb564-111">By requiring that travel expenses be approved before they are incurred, the organization can help make sure that the project remains within budget.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eb564-112">Reisiplaanide poliitikaid saate seadistada lehel **Reisiplaan**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb564-112">You can set up policies for travel requisitions from the **Travel requisition** page.</span></span>
