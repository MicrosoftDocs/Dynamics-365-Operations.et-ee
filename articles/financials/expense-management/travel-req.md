[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="e7de0-105">*Reisiplaan* on lähtedokument, mis loetleb reisimise eesmärgil tekkinud kulud.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7de0-105">A *travel requisition* is a source document that lists the expenses that will be incurred for the purpose of travel.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e7de0-106">Reisiplaan esitatakse ülevaatamiseks ja seejärel saab seda kasutada kulude kinnitamiseks.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7de0-106">A travel requisition is submitted for review and can then be used to authorize expenses.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e7de0-107">Teie organisatsioon võib nõuda reisiplaani esitamist enne kui töötajad teevad kulutusi, mille eest tasub organisatsioon.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7de0-107">Your organization might require that a travel requisition be submitted before employees incur any expense that is charged to the organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e7de0-108">See nõue kehtib olenemata sellest, kas töötaja maksab ettevõtte krediitkaardiga, kasutab sularaha ettemakse kaudu saadud sularaha või tasub tegelike kulude eest, mille hüvitab organisatsioon.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7de0-108">This requirement applies, regardless of whether employees charge expenses to a corporate credit card, spend cash that they received from a cash advance, or incur out-of-pocket expenses that will be reimbursed by the organization.</span></span>
