# <a name="post-an-expense-report"></a><span data-ttu-id="bd321-103">Kuluaruande sisestamine</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bd321-103">Post an expense report</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="bd321-104">Pärast kuluaruande kinnitamist ja ülekandmist päevaraamatusse saab kuluaruande sisestada pearaamatusse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bd321-104">After an expense report has been approved and transferred to the general journal, it can be posted to the general ledger.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="bd321-105">Kuluaruande sisestamisel tuvastatakse kulud, mis on sobilikud käibemaksu korvamiseks.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bd321-105">When you post an expense report, expenses that are eligible for recovery of value-added tax (VAT) are identified.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="bd321-106">KM-i maksete kinnitamine ja tagastamine määratakse töövõtjale, kes vastutab kuluaruande kinnitamise eest.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bd321-106">The task of verifying and recovering VAT payments is assigned to the employee who is responsible for verifying the expense report.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="bd321-107">Kui kuluaruande kulude arve esitatakse muule ettevõttele kui ettevõttele, kus töövõtja töötab, peate kinnitama ettevõtte, kellele need kulud võlgnetakse, ja ettevõtte, kes peab kulud hüvitama.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bd321-107">If expenses on an expense report are charged to a company other than the company that employs the employee, you must verify the company that those expenses are owed to and the company that the expenses are owed from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="bd321-108">Näiteks töötab kuluaruande esitanud töövõtja ettevõttele DAT, kuid kulu eest esitati arve ettevõttele DIR.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bd321-108">For example, the employee who submitted an expense report works for the DAT company but charged an expense to the DIR company.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="bd321-109">Sellisel juhul on DAT ettevõte, kellele kulud võlgnetakse, ja DIR ettevõte, kes peab kulud hüvitama.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bd321-109">In this case, DAT is the company that the expense is owed to, and DIR is the company that the expense is owed from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="bd321-110">Pärast nende töölehe ridade kinnitamist saate kuluread pearaamatusse sisestada.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bd321-110">After you verify these journal lines, you can post the expense lines to the general ledger.</span></span>
