<a name="letters-of-credit"></a><span data-ttu-id="2d34f-107">Akreditiivid</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2d34f-107">Letters of credit</span></span>
-----------------

<span data-ttu-id="2d34f-108">Akreditiive kasutatakse rahvusvaheliste kannete puhul ja need aitavad tagada, et maksete tegemise.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2d34f-108">Letters of credit are used for international transactions and help guarantee that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2d34f-109">Akreditiiv on panga väljastatud lepe, millega pank nõustub ostja nimel makse tagama, tingimusel, et müüja ja ostja vahel sõlmitud leppe tingimused on täidetud.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2d34f-109">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to guarantee payment on behalf of a buyer, provided that the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2d34f-110">Akreditiivi nimetatakse ka dokumendikrediidiks (DC).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2d34f-110">A letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span>

## <a name="import-collections"></a><span data-ttu-id="2d34f-123">Impordi sissenõudmised</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2d34f-123">Import collections</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="2d34f-124">Impordi sissenõue on panga ja eksportija (müüja) vaheline lepe, millega pank nõustub saadetise dokumendid rahvusvahelisele importijale (ostja) tarnima.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2d34f-124">An import collection is an agreement between the bank and the exporter (seller), in which the bank agrees to deliver the shipping documentation to the international importer (buyer).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2d34f-125">Pangalt eeldatakse saadetise dokumentide tarnimist saadetavate kaupade makse saamisel sularahas või makse allkirjastatud kinnituse laekumisel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2d34f-125">The bank is expected to deliver the shipping documentation upon receipt of payment for the shipped goods in cash, or upon receipt of a signed draft toward the payment.</span></span>
