# <a name="allocate-a-project-budget-or-budget-revision-across-periods"></a><span data-ttu-id="33f39-103">Projekti eelarve või eelarve muutmise eraldamine perioodidele</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-103">Allocate a project budget or budget revision across periods</span></span>

[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="33f39-104"> Kui olete sisestanud projektieelarve või projekti eelarve revisjoni, siis enne selle kinnitamiseks edastamist saate summad perioodide vahel eraldada.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-104">After you enter a project budget or project budget revision, and before you submit it for approval, you can allocate the amounts across periods.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="33f39-105">Kui eelarve või eelarve revisjon on tagasi lükatud, saate eraldada summad enne selle uuesti edastamist.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-105">If the budget or budget revision is rejected, you can also allocate the amounts before you resubmit it.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="33f39-106">Pärast eelarve või eelarve revisjoni eraldamist saate eraldatud summad üle vaadata ja korrigeerida neid enne eelarve või eelarve revisjoni edastamist kinnitamistöövoogu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-106">After a budget or budget revision has been allocated, you can review the allocation amounts and make adjustments to them before you submit the budget or budget revision to the approval workflow.</span></span> 

1. <span data-ttu-id="33f39-107">Klõpsake suvandeid Projektihaldus ja raamatupidamine > Üldine > Projektid > Kõik projektid.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-107">Click Project management and accounting > Common > Projects > All projects.</span></span> 
2. <span data-ttu-id="33f39-108">Valige või avage projekt, mille jaoks eelarvet või eelarve revisjoni eraldada soovite.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-108">Select or open the project for which you want to allocate the budget or budget revision.</span></span> 
3. <span data-ttu-id="33f39-109">Klõpsake toimingupaanil vahekaardi Plaan grupis Eelarve suvandit Projekti eelarve.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-109">On the Action Pane, on the Plan tab, in the Budget group, click Project budget.</span></span> 
4. <span data-ttu-id="33f39-110">Klõpsake vormi Projekti eelarve toimingupaani vahekaardil Eelarve valikul Eralda eelarve.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-110">In the Project budget form, on the Action Pane, on the Budget tab, click Allocate budget.</span></span> 
5. <span data-ttu-id="33f39-111">Vormi Eelarve eraldamine väljadele Eraldamise lähtekoht ja Eraldamise sihtkoht sisestage esimene ja viimane kuupäev, mida soovite eelarve eraldamisse kaasata.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-111">In the Allocate budget form, in the Allocate from and Allocate to fields, enter the first and last dates that you want to include in the budget allocation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="33f39-112">Need on tavaliselt kogu projekti esimene ja viimane kuupäev või jooksva aasta esimene ja viimane kuupäev.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-112">These are usually either the first and last dates of the whole project, or the first and last dates of the current year.</span></span>  
   > <span data-ttu-id="33f39-113">[MÄRKUS.] Pärast kande tüübi eraldamist ja summade kinnitamist ei saa eraldamise ajaperioodi tuleviku eelarve revisjonides muuta.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-113">[NOTE!] After a transaction type has been allocated and the amounts approved, the allocation time period cannot be changed in future budget revisions.</span></span> 
6. <span data-ttu-id="33f39-114">Väljal Ajaperioodi alusel valige, kas soovite eraldada projekti eelarve aasta, kvartali või ettevõtte aastasisese rahandusperioodi järgi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-114">In the By time period field, select whether you want to allocate the project budget by year, quarter, or company fiscal periods within the year.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="33f39-115">Valige eraldamiseks kandetüübid ja klõpsake seejärel nuppu Eralda.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-115">Select the transaction types to allocate, and then click Allocate.</span></span> 
8. <span data-ttu-id="33f39-116">Vormil Projekti eelarve eraldamine või Projekti eelarve revisjoni eraldamine vaadake eraldatud summad üle või värskendage neid.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-116">In the Project budget allocation form or the Project budget revision allocation form, review or update the amounts that are allocated.</span></span> 
9. <span data-ttu-id="33f39-117">Sulgege leht.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-117">Close the page.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="33f39-118">Kui eraldamine on selline, nagu soovite, klõpsake lehel Projekti eelarve käsku Edasta, et saata eelarve või eelarve revisjonid kinnitamistöövoogu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33f39-118">When the allocation is the way that you want it, click Submit in the Project budget page to send the budget or budget revisions to the approval workflow.</span></span>
