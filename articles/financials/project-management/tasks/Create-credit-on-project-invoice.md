[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

1. <span data-ttu-id="d66d1-104">Avage Projektihaldus ja -arvestus > Projektid > Kõik projektid.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d66d1-104">Go to Project management and accounting > Projects > All projects.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="d66d1-106">Valige projekti ID, mida kreeditarve jaoks kasutatakse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d66d1-106">Select the Project ID that is used for the Credit Note.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d66d1-107">Klõpsake loendis valitud real olevat linki.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d66d1-107">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>

10. <span data-ttu-id="d66d1-115">Klõpsake nuppu OK.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d66d1-115">Click OK.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="d66d1-116">Pärast seda toimingut saab on kreeditarve arve soovituse jaoks saadaval.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d66d1-116">After this step, the Credit Note will be available for Invoice Proposal.</span></span>
