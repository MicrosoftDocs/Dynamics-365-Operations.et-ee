<span data-ttu-id="11531-115">Rakenduses Finance and Operations luuakse nii äri-/organisatsioonikliendid kui ka tarbijad/lõppkasutajad ühed tabelis nimetusega **CustTable** (CustomerCustomerV3Entity) ja neid liigitatakse atribuudi **Tüüp** järgi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="11531-115">In Finance and Operations, both commercial/organizational customers and consumers/end users are mastered in a single table that is named **CustTable** (CustomerCustomerV3Entity), and they are classified based on the **Type** attribute.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="11531-116">(Kui atribuudiks **Tüüp** on määratud **Organisatsioon**, on klient äri-/organisatsiooniklient ja kui atribuudiks **Tüüp** on määratud **Isik**, on klient tarbija/lõppkasutaja.) Esmase kontaktisiku teavet töödeldakse olemi SMMContactPersonEntity kaudu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="11531-116">(If **Type** is set to **Organization**, the customer is a commercial/organizational customer, and if **Type** is set to **Person**, the customer is a consumer/end user.) The primary contact person information is handled through the SMMContactPersonEntity entity.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="11531-117">Rakenduses Common Data Service luuakse äri-/organisatsioonikliendid olemis Konto ja tuvastatakse klientidena, kui atribuudiks **Seose tüüp** on määratud **Klient**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="11531-117">In Common Data Service, commercial/organizational customers are mastered in the Account entity and are identified as customers when the **RelationshipType** attribute is set to **Customer**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="11531-118">Nii tarbijaid/lõppkasutajaid kui ka kontaktisikut esindab olem Kontakt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="11531-118">Both consumers/end users and the contact person are represented by the Contact entity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="11531-119">Et tagada tarbija/lõppkasutaja ja kontaktisiku selge eristamine, on olemil**Kontakt** loogikalipp nimega **Müüdav**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="11531-119">To provide a clear separation between a consumer/end user and a contact person, the **Contact** entity has a Boolean flag that is named **Sellable**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="11531-120">Kui **Müüdav** on **Tõene**, on kontakt tarbija/lõppkasutaja ja selle kontakt jaoks saab luua pakkumisi ja tellimusi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="11531-120">When **Sellable** is **True**, the contact is a consumer/end user, and quotations and orders can be created for that contact.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="11531-121">Kui **Müüdav** on **Väär**, on kontakt vaid kliendi esmane kontaktisik.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="11531-121">When **Sellable** is **False**, the contact is just a primary contact person of a customer.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="11531-122">Kui mittemüüdav kontakt osaleb pakkumise või tellimuse toimingus, on atribuudiks **Müüdav** määratud **Tõene**, et märkida kontakt lipuga müüdava kontaktina.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="11531-122">When a non-sellable contact participates in a quotation or order process, **Sellable** is set to **True** to flag the contact as a sellable contact.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="11531-123">Kontakt, kes on saanud müüdavaks kontaktiks, jääb müüdavaks kontaktiks.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="11531-123">A contact that has become a sellable contact remains a sellable contact.</span></span>
