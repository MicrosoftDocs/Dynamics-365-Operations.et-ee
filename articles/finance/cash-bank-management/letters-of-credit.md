[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="33997-104">Akreditiivid on pangadokumendid, mida kasutatakse tavaliselt kaupade ostmiseks ja müügiks välisriikides.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-104">Letters of credit are bank documents that are commonly used for the purchase and sale of goods across international borders.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="33997-105">Akreditiive kasutatakse rahvusvaheliste kannete puhul maksete tagamiseks.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-105">Letters of credit are used for international transactions to ensure that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="33997-106">Akreditiiv on panga väljastatud lepe, milles pank nõustub tagama makse ostja nimel, kui ostja ja müüja vahelise leppe tingimused on täidetud.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-106">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to ensure payment on behalf of the buyer, if the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="33997-107">Pange tähele, et akreditiivi nimetatakse ka dokumendikrediidiks (DC).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-107">Note that a letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="33997-108">Impordiakreditiivi puhul on juriidiline isik ostja või akreditiivi taotleja.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-108">For an import letter of credit, the legal entity is the buyer or the applicant for the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="33997-109">Ekspordiakreditiivi puhul on juriidiline isik müüja või akreditiivi kasusaaja.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-109">For an export letter of credit, the legal entity is the seller or the beneficiary of the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="33997-110">Akreditiivi on kaasatud järgmised osapooled.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-110">The following parties are involved with a letter of credit:</span></span> 

 - <span data-ttu-id="33997-111">Kandidaat (ostja), kes soovib kaupade eest maksta</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-111">The applicant (buyer) who intends to pay for the goods</span></span> 
 - <span data-ttu-id="33997-112">Kasusaaja (müüja), kes makse saab</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-112">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="33997-113">Väljaandev pank, mis akreditiivi väljastab</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-113">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="33997-114">Nõustav pank, mis kandidaadi nimel kande täidab</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-114">The advising bank that carries out the transaction on behalf of the applicant</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="33997-115">Akreditiiv sisaldab kaupade kirjeldust, vajalikke dokumente, saatmise kuupäeva ja aegumiskuupäeva, misjärel makset ei tehta.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-115">The letter of credit includes a description of the goods, any required documents, the date of shipment, and the expiration date after which payment will not be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="33997-116">Väljaandev pank võtab akreditiivi eest marginaali.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-116">The issuing bank collects a margin for the letter of credit.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="33997-117">Akreditiiv võib olla kas tühistatav või mittetühistatav.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-117">A letter of credit can be revocable or irrevocable.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="33997-118">Akreditiiv võib olla edastatav, mitte-edastatav või seostuv.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-118">The nature of a letter of credit can be transferable, non transferable, or revolving.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="33997-119">Tavaliselt on akreditiiv tühistamatu ja kinnitatud kokkulepe, et konkreetsele kasusaajale tehakse makse täielike ja korrektsete transpordidokumentide esitamisel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="33997-119">Typically, a letter of credit is an irrevocable and confirmed agreement that payment will be made to a specific beneficiary upon submission of complete and accurate shipping documentation.</span></span>
