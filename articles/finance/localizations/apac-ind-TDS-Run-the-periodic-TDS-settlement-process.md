# <a name="run-the-periodic-tds-settlement-process"></a><span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-103">Perioodilise TDS-i tasakaalustusprotsessi käitamine</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-103">Run the periodic TDS settlement process</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-104">See teema kirjeldab, kuidas tasakaalustada allikas (TDS) mahaarvatud perioodilist maksu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-104">This topic explains how to settle periodic Tax Deducted at Source (TDS).</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-105">Minge **Maksu \> Deklaratsioonidesse \> Kinnipeetav maks \> Kinnipeetava maksu makse**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-105">Go to **Tax \> Declarations \> Withholding tax \> Withholding tax payment**.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-106">[![Kinnipeetava maksu makse dialoogiboks](./media/apac-ind-TDS-47.png)](./media/apac-ind-TDS-47.png)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-106">[![Withholding tax payment dialog box](./media/apac-ind-TDS-47.png)](./media/apac-ind-TDS-47.png)</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-107">Dialoogiaknas **Kinnipeetava maksu makse** väljal **Maksu tüüp** valige **TDS**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-107">In the **Withholding tax payment** dialog box, in the **Tax type** field, select **TDS**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-108">Väljal **Maksukonto number (TAN)** valige maksukonto number (TAN), mille tasakaalustusprotsessi käitada.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-108">In the **Tax Account Number (TAN)** field, select the Tax Account Number (TAN) to run the settlement process for.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-109">Väljal **Kinnipeetava maksu komponendigrupp** valige TDS-i komponendigrupp, mille tasakaalustusprotsess käivitada.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-109">In the **Withholding tax component group** field, select the TDS component group to run the settlement process for.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-110">Väljal **Tasakaalustusperiood** valige TDS-i tasakaalustusperiood, mille tasakaalustusprotsessi käivitada.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-110">In the **Settlement period** field, select the TDS settlement period to run the settlement process for.</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]
    > <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-111">TDS-i tasakaalustusprotsessi käitatakse kõigi perioodide kohta, mis on seadistatud TDS-i tasakaalustusperioodiks lehekülje **Perioodid** vahekaardil **Kinnipeetava maksu tasakaalustusperioodid** lehel (**Maksud \> Kaudsed maksud \> Kinnipeetav maks \> kinnipeetav maksu tasakaalustusperioodid**).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-111">The TDS settlement process is run for all periods that are set up for the TDS settlement period on the **Periods** tab of the **Withholding tax settlement periods** page (**Tax \> Indirect taxes \> Withholding tax \> Withholding tax settlement periods**).</span></span>

6. <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-112">Väljal **Kuupäevast** valige TDS-i tasakaalustusprotsessi käivitamiseks alguskuupäev.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-112">In the **From date** field, select the start date to run the TDS settlement process from.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-113">Tasakaalustusperioodi kindla perioodi alguskuupäevaks võetakse "alates" kuupäev.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-113">For a specific period within the settlement period, the start date that is defined for the period is taken as the "from" date.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-114">Näiteks TDS-i tasakaalustusperioodil on kaks perioodi: 1. aprill kuni 30. aprill 2009 ja 1. mai kuni 31. mai 2009.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-114">For example, the TDS settlement period has two periods: April 1 through April 30, 2009, and May 1 through May 31, 2009.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-115">Kui valite **04.06.2009** (6. aprill 2009) **alguskuupäevaks** käivitatakse tasakaalustusprotsess siiski alates 1. aprillist 2009.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-115">If you select **04/06/2009** (April 6, 2009) as the start date in the **From date** field, the settlement process will still run from April 1, 2009.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-116">Kui sisestate hilisema perioodi väljale **Kuupäevast** kuid tasakaalustamata tasakaalustusperioodi eelmist perioodi, siis tasakaalustust ei toimu eelmiste perioodide puhul.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-116">If you enter a later period in the **From date** field, but without settling a previous period within the settlement period, the settlement won't occur for any previous periods.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-117">Näiteks TDS-i tasakaalustusperioodil on kolm perioodi: 1. aprill kuni 30. aprill 2009, 1. mai kuni 31. mai 2009 ja 1. juuni kuni 30. juuni 2009.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-117">For example, the TDS settlement period has three periods: April 1 through April 30, 2009, May 1 through May 31, 2009, and June 1 through June 30, 2009.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-118">Kui valite **05.01.2009** (1. mai 2009) alguskuupäevaks väljal **Alates kuupäevast**, käivitatakse tasakaalustusprotsess ainult 1. maist kuni 31. maini 2009.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-118">If you select **05/01/2009** (May 1, 2009) as the start date in the **From date** field, the settlement process will run only from May 1 through May 31, 2009.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-119">Tasakaalustus ei toimu 01. aprillist kuni 30. aprillini 2009.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-119">The settlement won't occur for April 1 through April 30, 2009.</span></span>

7. <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-120">Väljal **Tasakaalustusprotsessi kuupäev** valige TDS-i tasakaalustusprotsessi käivitamiseks alguskuupäev.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-120">In the **Transaction date** field, select the date to post the TDS settlement transaction.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-121">Dialoogiaknas **Kinnipeetava maksu makse** väljal valige TDS tasakaaluversioon:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-121">In the **Withholding tax payment version** field, select the TDS settlement version:</span></span>

     - <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-122">**Originaal** – Kasutage seda valikut TDS-i tasakaalustusprotsessi esmakordselt käivitamiseks.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-122">**Original** – Use this option to run the TDS settlement process for the first time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-123">Algset makseversiooni kasutatakse vaid üks kord TDS-i tasakaalustusprotsessi käivitamiseks kinnipeetava maksu komponendigrupi ja kinnipeetava maksu tasakaalustusperioodi kombinatsiooni puhul.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-123">The original payment version is used only one time to run the TDS settlement process for a combination of a TAN, a withholding tax component group, and a withholding tax settlement period.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-124">**Viimased versioonid** – Kasutage seda valikut, kui TDS-i tasakaalustusprotsess on juba määratud perioodiks käitatud.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-124">**Latest versions** – Use this option if the TDS settlement process has already been run for the specified period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-125">Kaasab järelkuupäevaga kanded, mis sisestati pärast selle perioodi tasakaalustusprotsessi eelnevalt käivitamist.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-125">Include back-dated transactions that were posted after the settlement process was previously run for the period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-126">Selle valiku abil saate tasakaalustusprotsessi käivitada mis tahes arv kordi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-126">You can use this option to run the settlement process any number of times.</span></span>

9. <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-127">Valige **Uuenda** märkeruut TDS-i tasakaalustusprotsessi käivitamiseks ja summade pearaamatukontodele sisestamiseks.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-127">Select the **Update** check box to run the TDS settlement process and post the amounts to the ledger accounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-128">Kui see ruut on tühi, siis tasakaalustusprotsessi ei käivitata ja finantskandeid ei looda.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-128">If this check box is cleared, the settlement process won't be run, and the financial entries won't be generated.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-129">Valige **OK** TDS-i tasakaalustusprotsessi käivitamiseks ja kinnipeetava maksu maksearuande loomiseks.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-129">Select **OK** to run the TDS settlement process and generate the withholding tax payment report.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-130">Tasakaalustusprotsessi kaasatud TDS-kannete olek kuvatakse **Tasakaalustusena** **Tasakaalustus** lehel (minge **Ostureskontro \> Maksete \> Hankija makse tööleht**, **Read**, valige **Funktsioonid** ja siis valige **Tasakaalustamine**).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-130">The status of TDS transactions that are included in the settlement process is shown as **Settled** on the **Settlement** page (go to **Accounts payable \> Payments \> Vendor payment journal**, select **Lines**, select **Functions**, and then select **Settlement**).</span></span>

### <a name="important-points"></a><span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-131">Olulised punktid</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-131">Important points</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-132">Kui kinnipeetava maksu komponendigruppi tasakaalustusprotsessi käigus ei valita, toimub tasakaalustus kõigi kinnipeetava maksu komponendigruppide puhul valitud TAN maksu ja tasakaalustusperioodi jooksul.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-132">If a withholding tax component group isn't selected during the settlement process, the settlement occurs for all the withholding tax component groups for the selected TAN and settlement period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-133">Iga kinnipeetava maksu komponendi rühma jaoks luuakse eraldi rida **Ava tehingu redigeerimine** lehel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-133">A separate line is created for each withholding tax component group on the **Open transaction editing** page.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-134">Tasakaalustusprotsess põhineb tasakaalustusperioodi hinnakategooria olemusel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-134">The settlement process is based on the nature of assessee category for a settlement period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-135">Kanded, mille hindamiskategooria olemus on **Ettevõte** tasakaalustatakse ja kuvatakse ühe reana **Avatud kannete redigeerimise** lehel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-135">Transactions where the nature of assessee category is **Company** are settled and are shown as one line on the **Open transaction editing** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-136">Kanded, mille hindamiskategooria olemus on **Ettevõte** tasakaalustatakse ja kuvatakse ühe reana **Avatud kannete redigeerimise** lehel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-136">Transactions where the nature of assessee is something other than **Company** are settled and are shown as one line on the **Open transaction editing** page.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-137">TDS-i tasakaalustatud TDS-kande ridade tähtaeg **Avatud kande redigeerimise** lehel on määratud TDS-i maksetingimustel, mis on TDS hankija jaoks määratud **Hankijad** lehel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-137">The due date for the settled TDS transaction lines on the **Open transaction editing** page is based on the terms of payment that are defined for the TDS authority vendor on the **Vendors** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2eaaf-138">Kui TDS-i asutuse hankija jaoks ei ole maksetingimusi määratletud, kuvatakse tasakaalustusperioodi viimane päev tähtajana.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eaaf-138">If the terms of payments aren't defined for the TDS authority vendor, the last day of the settlement period is shown as the due date.</span></span>
