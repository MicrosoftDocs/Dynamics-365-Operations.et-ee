[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="bb53b-104">See teema kirjeldab, kuidas vaadata allika (TDS) maksetes mahaarvatud makse ja kandeid, mis sisestati tasakaalustusperioodi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-104">This topic explains how to view the Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) payments and transactions that were posted for a settlement period.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-105">Avage **Maks \> Kaudsed maksud \> Kinnipeetav maks \> Kinnipeetava maksu tasakaalustusperioodid**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-105">Go to **Tax \> Indirect taxes \> Withholding tax \> Withholding tax settlement periods**.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-106">[![Käibemaksu tasakaalustusperioodide leht](./media/apac-ind-TDS-50.png)](./media/apac-ind-TDS-50.png)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-106">[![Withholding tax settlement periods page](./media/apac-ind-TDS-50.png)](./media/apac-ind-TDS-50.png)</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-107">Lehel **Kinnipeetava maksu tasakaalustusperioodid** valige **Kinnipeetava maksu maksed** et avada **Kinnipeetava maksu maksete** leht, kus kus saate vaadata konkreetse TDS-i tasakaalustusperioodi jaoks tehtud TDS-tasakaalustusi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-107">On the **Withholding tax settlement periods** page, select **Withholding tax payments** to open the **Withholding tax payments** page, where you can view the TDS settlements that were made for a specific TDS settlement period.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-108">Vahekaardil **Ülevaade** sisestage järgmine teave:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-108">The **Overview** tab shows the following information:</span></span>

    - <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-109">**Kuupäev** – käibemaksu tasakaalustuskuupäev.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-109">**Date** – The date of TDS settlement.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-110">**Kviitung** - Kuvatakse TDS tehingukande kviitungi number.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-110">**Voucher** – The voucher number of the TDS settlement transaction.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-111">**Maksu tüüp**– Maksu tüüp, mille puhul tasakaalustusprotsessi käitatakse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-111">**Tax type** – The tax type that the settlement process is run for.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-112">**Maksukonto number (TAN)** – Arveldatud TDS-kande maksukonto number (TAN).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-112">**Tax Account Number (TAN)** – The Tax Account Number (TAN) of the settled TDS transaction.</span></span>

    - <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-114">**Alguskuupäev** – Tasakaalustusperioodi algus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-114">**From date** – The start date of the settlement period.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-115">**Kuni kuupäevani** – Tasakaalustusperioodi lõpu kuupäev.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-115">**To date** – The end date of the settlement period.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-116">**Kinnipeetava maksu makse versioon** – Kinnipeetava maksu makse versioon: **Algne**, **Viimased parandused** või **Kogu nimekiri**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-116">**Withholding tax payment version** – The version of the withholding tax payment: **Original**, **Latest corrections**, or **Total list**.</span></span>

5. <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-117">Valige **Kanne** et vaaddata TDS-kande kandekirjeid.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-117">Select **Voucher** to view the voucher entries for the TDS transaction.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-118">Valige **Kinnipeetava maksu kanded** et vaadata TDS-kannete üksikasju, mis tasakaalustusperioodi jooksul konkreetsel perioodil tasakaalustati.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-118">Select **Withholding tax transactions** to view the details of the TDS transactions that were settled for a specific period during a settlement period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-119">Valige **Kanne** et vaaddata TDS-kande kandekirjeid.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-119">Select **Voucher** to view the voucher entries for the TDS transaction.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-120">Valige **Kinnipeetava maksu komponendid** et avada kinnipeetava maksu komponentide leht, kus saate vaadataTDS-i, mis arvutati konkreetse TDS-i maksukoodi kohta.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-120">Select **Withholding tax components** to view the TDS that was calculated per TDS tax component for a specific TDS tax code.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-121">Sulgege **Kinnipeetava maksu maksete** leht, et naasta **Kinnipeetava maksu tasakaalustusperioodide** lehele.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-121">Close the **Withholding tax payments** page to return to the **Withholding tax settlement periods** page.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="bb53b-122">Valige **Kinnipeetava maksu kanded** et vaadata TDS-kannete üksikasju, mis tasakaalustusperioodi jooksul konkreetsel perioodil tasakaalustati.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb53b-122">Select **Withholding tax transactions** to view the details of the TDS transactions that were settled for the settlement period.</span></span>
