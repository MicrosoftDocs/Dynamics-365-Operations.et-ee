# <a name="adjust-leases"></a><span data-ttu-id="6e110-104">Rendikirjete korrigeerimine</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-104">Adjust leases</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="6e110-105">Teema selgitab, kuidas korrigeerida rendikirjet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-105">The topic explains how to adjust a lease.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-106">Korrigeerimine võib olla vajalik, kui rendiperioodi muudetakse, renti pikendatakse või muud asjaolud muutuvad.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-106">Adjustment might be required if the lease terms are modified, the lease is extended, or other circumstances change.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-107">Vara rentimine vastab raamatupidamise standardite kodeerimise teema 842 (ASC 842) ja rahvusvahelise finantsaruandluse standardi 16 (IFRS 16) ette nähtud rendikirje muutmise juhistele.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-107">Asset leasing complies with the guidance that Accounting Standards Codification Topic 842 (ASC 842) and International Financial Reporting Standard 16 (IFRS 16) provide about lease modifications.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-108">ASC-842-20-15-1 määratleb rendikirje muudatused kui mis tahe lepingutingimuse muudatuse, mis põhjustab rendi ulatuse ja tasu muudatuse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-108">ASC 842-20-15-1 defines a lease modification as any change to the terms and conditions of a contract that causes a change in the scope of, or the consideration for, a lease.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-109">IFRS 16 lõikes 39 on sätestatud, et rentnik peab rendikohustise uuesti hindama, et see vastaks rendimaksete muudatustele.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-109">Paragraph 39 of IFRS 16 states that a lessee must revalue the lease liability so that it reflects changes to the lease payments.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6e110-110">Organisatsioonide puhul, mis järgivad standardeid ASC 842 või IFRS 16, arvestatakse rent uuesti, et kajastada tulevaste rendimaksete minimaalse eelseatud väärtuse (PVFMLP) muudatus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-110">For organizations that adhere to ASC 842 or IFRS 16, a lease is remeasured to reflect a change in the present value of the future minimum lease payments (PVFMLP).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-111">Kui PVFMLP suureneb, debiteeritakse loodav töölehe kirje kasutamisõiguse esemeks oleva vara ja krediteeritakse rendikohustise erinevust uue PVFMLP ja eelmise PVFMLP vahel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-111">If the PVFMLP increases, the journal entry that is created will be a debit to the right-of-use (ROU) asset and a credit to the lease liability for the difference between the new PVFMLP and the previous PVFMLP.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-112">Kui PVFMLP väheneb, debiteeritakse rendikohustise töölehe kirje ja krediteeritakse kasutusõiguse esemeks olev vara erinevus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-112">If the PVFMLP decreases, the journal entry will be a debit to the lease liability and a credit to the ROU asset for the difference.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6e110-113">Kui korrigeerimine vähendab kasutamisõiguse esemeks olev vara nii, et see möödub nullist (0), krediteeritakse ülejäänud summa liisingu sisestamise kontol, mis on määratud lehel **Rendi sisestamise kontod**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-113">If the adjustment decreases the ROU asset past 0 (zero), the remainder will be credited to the gain on lease modification posting account that is specified on the **Lease posting accounts** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-114">Süsteem arvestab nende kannete ja muude korrigeerimise annetega nt klassifikatsiooni muudatused esmased otsekulutused, rendistiimulid, eelmaksed ja demonteerimiskulud, mis tulenevad rendi muudatustest.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-114">The system accounts for these transactions and other adjustment entries, such as classification changes, initial direct costs, lease incentives, prepayments, and dismantling costs that arise from lease modifications.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6e110-115">Rendi muutmise tehingute konkreetseteks juhisteks soovitame vaadata IFRS 16 ja ASC 842.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-115">For specific guidance about lease adjustment transactions, we recommend that you see IFRS 16 and ASC 842.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6e110-116">Rendi muutmiseks tehke järgmist.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-116">To adjust a lease, follow these steps.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-117">Muudetud andmed värskendatakse rendigraafikutes pärast toimingu Loo graafik käivitamist.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-117">The modified data will update lease schedules after the Create schedule process is run.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="6e110-120">Lehel **Rendikirje korrigeerimine** sisestage korrigeeritud rendikirje jaoks uus teave.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-120">On the **Adjust lease** page, enter the new information for the adjusted lease.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="6e110-121">Pange tähele, et leht **Rendikirje korrigeerimine** sarnaneb lehele **Rendikirje lisamine**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-121">Notice that the **Adjust lease** page resembles the **Add lease** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-122">Lisaks sarnaselt rendikirje lisamisel määratletav alguskuupäev teeb kindlaks uue rendi alguse, määratleb väli **Muutmise kuupäev** lehel **Rendikirje korrigeerimine** muudetud rendiperioodi algusaja.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-122">Additionally, just as the commencement date that you specify when you add a lease determines when the new lease starts, the **Modification date** field on the **Adjust lease** page determines when the modified lease starts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-123">Seda kuupäev ei saa määrata hilisemaks kui maksegraafiku rea alguskuupäev.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-123">This date can't be after the start date on the payment schedule lines.</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]
    > <span data-ttu-id="6e110-124">Rendi korrigeerimistele rakenduvad väljad **Kasutusõiguse aluseks olevad kaalutlused**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-124">The **ROU considerations** fields apply to the lease adjustment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-125">Kui muudetud rendiga ei ole seotud esmaseid otsekulutusi, rendimakseid, ettemakseid ega demonteerimiskulusid, jätke need väljad tühjaks.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-125">If no initial direct costs, lease incentives, prepayments, or dismantling costs are associated with the modified lease, you should leave these fields blank.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-126">Algsed summad ei kohaldu värskendatud rendile.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-126">The original amounts won't apply to the updated lease.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-127">Kõik lisakulud, mis rendi muutmise ajal tekivad, tuleb sisestada lehel **Rendikirje korrigeerimine**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-127">Any additional costs that are incurred when the lease is modified should be entered on the **Adjust lease** page.</span></span>
    > 
    > <span data-ttu-id="6e110-128">Näiteks annab rendileandja rendi pikendamis kokkuleppe eest 1000 eurose soodustuse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-128">For example, a lessor provides a $1,000 incentive for agreeing to a lease extension.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-129">Sel juhul, kui korrigeerite renti pikendamise konto jaoks, sisestate väljale **Rendistiimulid** summa **1000**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-129">In this case, when you adjust the lease to account for the extension, you enter **1,000** in the **Lease incentives** field.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-130">Kui rendi korrigeerimisega ei seostata ühtegi stiimulit, peate tühjendama mis tahes eelnevalt sellele väljale sisestatud väärtuse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-130">If no incentives are associated with the lease adjustment, you should clear any value that was previously entered in this field.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-131">Sama loogikat rakendatakse teistele kasutusõiguse aluseks olevatele kaalutlustele.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-131">The same logic is applied to other ROU considerations.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="6e110-132">Korrigeeritud rendi maksegraafiku read tuleb luua potentsiaalsuse alusel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-132">The payment schedule lines of the adjusted lease should be created on a prospective basis.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e110-133">Potentsiaalsuse alusel loodavad maksegraafiku read ei saa alata enne rendi korrigeerimiste jõustumist.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e110-133">Payment schedule lines that are created on a prospective basis can't start before the lease modifications take effect.</span></span>
