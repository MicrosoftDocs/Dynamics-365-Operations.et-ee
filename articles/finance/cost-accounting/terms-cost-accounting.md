<span data-ttu-id="d1c37-105">**Eraldamise alus**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1c37-105">**Allocation base**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="d1c37-106">Eraldamisalust kasutatakse tegevuste mõõtmiseks ja kvantifitseerimiseks, nt kasutatavad masina töötunnid, tarbitavad kilovatt-tunnid (kWh) või kasutatav pindala.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1c37-106">The allocation base is used to measure and quantify activities, such as machine hours that are used, kilowatt hours that are consumed, or square footage that is occupied.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d1c37-107">Seda kasutatakse ühele või mitmele kuluobjektile kulude eraldamise alusena</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1c37-107">It's used as basis for allocating costs to one or more cost objects</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="d1c37-108">**Kuluarvestus**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1c37-108">**Cost accounting**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="d1c37-109">Kuluarvestus võimaldab koguda andmeid erinevatest allikatest, näiteks pearaamatust, alammoodulist, eelarvetest ja statistilisest teabest.</span><span <span data-ttu-id="d1c37-115">**Kuluarvestuse pearaamat**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1c37-115">**Cost accounting ledger**</span></span>

-  <span data-ttu-id="d1c37-125">Osakonnad</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1c37-125">Departments</span></span>
-  <span data-ttu-id="d1c37-126">Kulukeskused</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1c37-126">Cost centers</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="d1c37-128">**Kuluelement**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1c37-128">**Cost element**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="d1c37-145">**Kulukäitumine**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d1c37-145">**Cost behavior**</span></span>
