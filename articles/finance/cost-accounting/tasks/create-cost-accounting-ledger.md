[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="97b28-104">Kuluarvestuse pearaamat tähistab üldist aruandlusüksust.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97b28-104">A cost accounting ledger represents the overall reporting unit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="97b28-105">See on määratletud kuluelemendi dimensiooni, statistilise dimensiooni, rahanduskalendri ja valuuta.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97b28-105">It is defined by a cost element dimension, statistical dimension, fiscal calendar, and currency.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="97b28-106">See on juriidiliste isikute kontseptsioonist sõltumatu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97b28-106">It is agnostic to the concept of legal entities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="97b28-107">Juriidilise isiku ja tema andmed saab seostada mitme kuluarvestuse pearaamatuga.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97b28-107">A legal entity and its data can be associated with many cost accounting ledgers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="97b28-108">See salvestis kasutab USP2 demoandmete ettevõtet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97b28-108">This recording uses the USP2 demo data company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="97b28-109">Avage Kuluarvestus > Pearaamatu häälestamine > Kuluarvestuse pearaamatud.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97b28-109">Go to Cost accounting > Ledger setup > Cost accounting ledgers.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="97b28-110">Klõpsake valikut Uus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97b28-110">Click New.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="97b28-111">Sisestage väärtus väljale Nimi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97b28-111">In the Name field, type a value.</span></span>

5. <span data-ttu-id="97b28-113">Sisestage või valige väärtus väljal Rahandusaasta kalender.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97b28-113">In the Fiscal calendar field, enter or select a value.</span></span>

8. <span data-ttu-id="97b28-116">Sisestage või valige väärtus väljal Statistiline dimensioon.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97b28-116">In the Statistical dimension field, enter or select a value.</span></span>
9. <span data-ttu-id="97b28-117">Klõpsake nuppu Salvesta.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="97b28-117">Click Save.</span></span>
