- <span data-ttu-id="447bb-122">**Koguhindade võrdlemine rea kaubaga** – selline võrdlemine on kasulik ettevõtetele, kas saavad harilikult ühe ostutellimuse rea kohta mitu arvet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="447bb-122">**Price totals for line item matching** – This type of matching is useful for companies that typically receive multiple invoices for a single purchase order line.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="447bb-123">Kui saate ostutellimuse rea kohta tavaliselt ainult ühe arve, ei ole seda tüüpi võrdlemine vajalik.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="447bb-123">If you typically receive only one invoice per purchase order line, this type of matching is not necessary.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="447bb-124">See võrdlemine nõuab, et kahe- või kolmesuunaline võrdlemine on lubatud ja toimib mitteületatava netosumma kinnitamisena, põhinedes hälbeprotsendil ja -summadel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="447bb-124">This matching requires that two-way or three-way matching is enabled and serves as a not-to-exceed net amount validation, based on a tolerance percentages and amounts.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="447bb-125">Selline võrdlemine võrdleb iga arve rea ja kõikide ootel ja varem sisestatud arve ridade netosumma hinnateavet asjakohase ostutellimuse rea netosummaga.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="447bb-125">This type of matching compares price information for the net amount of each line on the invoice, and all pending and previously posted invoice lines, with the net amount of the corresponding purchase order line.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="447bb-126">Netosumma määratakse järgmise valemiga: (ühiku hind \* rea kogus) + rea tasud – rea allahindlused.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="447bb-126">The net amount is determined by the following formula: (Unit price \* Line quantity) + Line charges -
