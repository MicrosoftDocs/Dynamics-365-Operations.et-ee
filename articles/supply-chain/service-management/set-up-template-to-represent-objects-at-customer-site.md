# <a name="set-up-template-to-represent-objects-at-customer-site"></a><span data-ttu-id="316bb-103">Kliendi juures asuvaid objekte kajastava malli seadistamine</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="316bb-103">Set up template to represent objects at customer site</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="316bb-104">Kasutage seda protseduuri, et seadistada mall, mis tähistab kliendi saidi objekte.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="316bb-104">Use this procedure to set up a template that represents objects at a customer site.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="316bb-105">Klõpsake valikut **Hooldushaldus** \> **Üldine** \> **Hooldustellimused** \> **Hooldusleppegrupid**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="316bb-105">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="316bb-106">Topeltklõpsake teenuselepet või klõpsake valikut **Teenuselepe**, et luua uus teenuselepe.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="316bb-106">Double-click a service agreement, or click **Service agreement** to create a new service agreement.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="316bb-107">Klõpsake vahekaarti **Seadistus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="316bb-107">Click the **Setup** tab.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="316bb-108">Klõpsake nuppu **Teenuse objektid**, et siduda mallkooslus teenuseleppega.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="316bb-108">Click **Service objects** to attach a template bill of materials (BOM) to the service agreement.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="316bb-109">Valige vormil **Teenuse objektid** teenuse objekt ja klõpsake seejärel valikuid **Funktsioonid** \> **Lisa mallkooslus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="316bb-109">In the **Service objects** form, select a service object, and then click **Functions** \> **Attach template BOM**.</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="316bb-110">Vormis **Vali mallkooslus** valige mallkooslus väljal **Mallkooslus** ja klõpsake seejärel nuppu **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="316bb-110">In the **Select template BOM** form, select a template BOM in the **Template BOM** field, and then click **OK**.</span></span>

7.  <span data-ttu-id="316bb-111">Vormil **Teenuse objektid** klõpsake valikut **Kujundaja**, et muuta mallkoosluse read nii, et mallkooslus esindaks soovitud objekte.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="316bb-111">In the **Service objects** form, click **Designer** to modify the template BOM so that the BOM represents the objects that you want.</span></span>

8.  <span data-ttu-id="316bb-112">Muudatuste salvestamiseks sulgege vormid.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="316bb-112">Close the forms to save your changes.</span></span>
