1.  <span data-ttu-id="fa467-104">Klõpsake valikuid **Teenuste haldus** \> **Üldine** \> **Teenuse kordustellimused** \> **Kõik teenuse kordustellimused**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fa467-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>
