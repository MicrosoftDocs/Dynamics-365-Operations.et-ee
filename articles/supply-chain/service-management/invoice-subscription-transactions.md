# <a name="invoice-subscription-transactions"></a><span data-ttu-id="ea7c7-103">Kordustellimuste kannete arveldamine</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea7c7-103">Invoice subscription transactions</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]



1.  <span data-ttu-id="ea7c7-104">Klõpsake valikuid **Teenuste haldus** \> **Üldine** \> **Teenuse kordustellimused** \> **Kõik teenuse kordustellimused**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea7c7-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="ea7c7-105">Hoolduse kordustellimuse loomiseks valige hooldustellimus ja klõpsake siis nuppu **Redigeeri** või valikut **Kordustellimus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea7c7-105">Select a service subscription and then click **Edit** or click **Subscription** to create a service subscription.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="ea7c7-106">Klõpsake valikuid **Analüüs** \> **Tasukanded**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea7c7-106">Click **Analyze** \> **Fee transactions**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="ea7c7-107">Valige tasukanne või tasukanded, mida soovite arveldada, ja klõpsake siis vormi **Arvesoovitused** avamiseks valikuid **Funktsioonid** \> **Arvesoovitus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea7c7-107">Select the fee transaction or fee transactions to invoice, and then click **Functions** \> **Invoice proposal** to open the **Invoice proposals** form.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="ea7c7-108">Klõpsake valikut **Loo arve** ja sisestage soovitud parameetrid vormile **Arve koostamine**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea7c7-108">Click **Create invoice** and enter the appropriate parameters in the **Create invoice** form</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="ea7c7-109">Klõpsake arvesoovituse loomiseks vormil **Arvesoovitused** nuppu **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea7c7-109">Click **OK** to create the invoice proposal in the **Invoice proposals** form.</span></span>

7.  <span data-ttu-id="ea7c7-110">Kontrollige arve sisu ja klõpsake vormi **Arve sisestamine** avamiseks valikut **Sisesta arve**, et arve sisestada.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea7c7-110">Verify the contents of the invoice, click **Post invoice** to open the **Post invoice** form to post the invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ea7c7-111">Vormil **Arve sisestamine** saate arve ka printida.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea7c7-111">You can also print the invoice from the **Post invoice** form.</span></span>
