[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="dcf51-104">Saate mooduli **Projektihaldus ja raamatupidamine** projektist hoolduse kordustellimuse luua.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dcf51-104">You can create a service subscription from a project in **Project management and accounting**.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="dcf51-105">Klõpsake valikuid **Projektihaldus ja raamatupidamine** \> **Üldine** \> **Projektid** \> **Kõik projektid**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dcf51-105">Click **Project management and accounting** \> **Common** \> **Projects** \> **All projects**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="dcf51-106">Avage projekt, millele soovite tellimust luua.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dcf51-106">Open the project that you want to create a subscription for.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="dcf51-107">Klõpsake vormi **Projektid** **toimingupaanil** vahekaarti **Halda**, seejärel **Teenus** \> **Teenuse kordustellimus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dcf51-107">On the **Projects** form, on the **Action Pane**, on the **Manage** tab, click **Service** \> **Service subscription**.</span></span>

> <P><span data-ttu-id="dcf51-112">Kui olete määranud kordustellimuste grupile numbrijärjestuse, luuakse tellimusele automaatselt kood, kui määratlete kordustellimuste grupi.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dcf51-112">If you have set up a number sequence for the subscription group, a subscription ID is generated automatically for the subscription when you specify the subscription group.</span></span></P>
