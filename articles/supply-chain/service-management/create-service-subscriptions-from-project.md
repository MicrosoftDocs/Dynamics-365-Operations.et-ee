[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

2.  <span data-ttu-id="3cdf2-107">Klõpsake vormi **Projektid** **toimingupaanil** vahekaarti **Halda**, seejärel **Teenus** \> **Teenuse kordustellimus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3cdf2-107">On the **Projects** form, on the **Action Pane**, on the **Manage** tab, click **Service** \> **Service subscription**.</span></span>
