4. <span data-ttu-id="d8053-109">Vahekaardil **Puu juhtelement** laiendage puud, otsige üles mall, mis sisaldab ridasid, mida soovite kopeerida hooldusleppesse ja valige hooldusmall.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d8053-109">On the **Tree control** tab, expand the tree, find the template that contains the lines that you want to copy to the service agreement, and then select the service template.</span></span>

7. <span data-ttu-id="d8053-112">Muudatuste salvestamiseks sulgege lehed.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d8053-112">Close the pages to save your changes.</span></span>
