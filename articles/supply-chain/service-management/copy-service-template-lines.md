# <a name="copy-service-templates-lines"></a><span data-ttu-id="021e6-103">Hooldusmalli ridade kopeerimine</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="021e6-103">Copy service templates lines</span></span>

## <a name="copy-service-template-lines-into-a-service-agreement"></a><span data-ttu-id="021e6-105">Hooldusmalli ridade kopeerimine hooldusleppesse</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="021e6-105">Copy service template lines into a service agreement</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="021e6-106">Klõpsake valikut **Hoolduslepped**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="021e6-106">Click **Service agreements**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="021e6-107">Topeltklõpsake hoolduslepet, et avada leht **Hoolduslepped** .</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="021e6-107">Double-click a service agreement to open the **Service agreements** page.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="021e6-108">Klõpsake valikut **Malli read**, et avada leht **Hooldusmalli ridade kopeerimine**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="021e6-108">Click **Template lines** to open the **Copy service template lines** page.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="021e6-109">Vahekaardil **Puu juhtelement** laiendage puud, otsige üles mall, mis sisaldab ridasid, mida soovite kopeerida hooldusleppesse ja valige hooldusmall.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="021e6-109">On the **Tree control** tab, expand the tree, find the template that contains the lines that you want to copy to the service agreement, and then select the service template.</span></span>

6. <span data-ttu-id="021e6-111">Klõpsake valikut **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="021e6-111">Click **OK**.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="021e6-112">Muudatuste salvestamiseks sulgege lehed.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="021e6-112">Close the pages to save your changes.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="021e6-113">Hooldusmalli read koos seostatud hooldusobjekti ja hooldustoimingu seostega on nüüd hooldusleppesse kopeeritud.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="021e6-113">The service template lines are now copied to the service agreement, together with the attached service object and service task relations.</span></span>
