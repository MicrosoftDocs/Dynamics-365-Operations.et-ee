## <a name="start-time-recording"></a><span data-ttu-id="fbbee-105">Aja kirjendamise alustamine</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fbbee-105">Start time recording</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="fbbee-107">Klõpsake vahekaarti **Hooldustellimus**. **Toimingupaanil** grupis **Teenindustaseme leping** klõpsake valikut **Käivita**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fbbee-107">Click the **Service order** tab. ## <a name="stop-time-recording"></a><span data-ttu-id="fbbee-109">Peata kellaaja salvestamine</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fbbee-109">Stop time recording</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="fbbee-110">Klõpsake valikuid **Teenusehaldus** \> **Üldine** \> **Hooldustellimused** \> **Hooldustellimused**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fbbee-110">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service orders** \> **Service orders**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="fbbee-111">Klõpsake vahekaarti **Hooldustellimus**. **Toimingupaanil** grupis **Teenindustaseme leping** klõpsake valikut **Peata**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fbbee-111">Click the **Service order** tab.
