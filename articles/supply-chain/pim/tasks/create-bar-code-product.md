[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

1. <span data-ttu-id="2eec5-106">Valige kodulehel **Väljastatud toote hooldus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eec5-106">Select **Released product maintenance** on the homepage.</span></span>

10. <span data-ttu-id="2eec5-116">**Tippige väärtus väljale Vöötkood.**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eec5-116">In the **Bar code** field, type a value.</span></span>

13. <span data-ttu-id="2eec5-119">Sulgege leht.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2eec5-119">Close the page.</span></span>
